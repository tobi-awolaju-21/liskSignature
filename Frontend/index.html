Lisk Signature

"Eliminating the key dangers associated with AI-generated video with just 4 lines of codes."

The proliferation of prompt-generated video and deepfake technology raise several important considerations regarding their impact on society, privacy, and security. Such as:

  • Misinformation and Fake News
  • Identity Theft and Fraud
  • National Security Threats

What is our solution ?

Links Signature is an SDK , that creates a public ledger of shas of video frames on the public blockchain with other infos embedded such as the metadata from the video, info about the device being used, location, and other necessary parameters.

It makes it possible for anyone to validate the authenticity of a video by simply asking for the Link Signature and searching for the video on Lisk Scan. The level of video authenticity verification is only available to devices/apps that require and are using the Lisk SDK.

Technologies Used

  • Lisk: Our EVM-compatible Lisk signature smart contracts were deployed here.
  • Solidity: Our smart contracts were written in Solidity.
  • Hardhat: Used in deploying our smart contracts to Lisk Testnet2.
